Summary

David is an experienced developer, technology project manager, and recruiter. Prior to establishing Search Technology in 2005, David held Management, Director and Vice President leadership positions with Ernst & Young, J.P.Morgan Chase and other technology companies. After joining Search Services in 2005 to launch the Search Technology division, David has been instrumental in attracting and growing the recruiting team resulting in recognition as one of Houston’s Fast Tech 50 organizations over multiple years as selected by the Houston Business Journal.
